About Our Project

RosettaGames hosts a vast array of web-based games spanning diverse genres, from retro action and puzzles to strategic challenges and thrilling adventures. ðŸŽ¯ðŸ§©ðŸŽ­

Rosettagames is an exciting open-source repository that serves as a collaborative platform for the talented members of Rosetta-21 to come together and contribute their skills towards the creation of a remarkable website that hosts an impressive collection of engaging, JavaScript-based games. it aims to provide a valuable training ground where members of Rosetta-21 can enhance their skills, deepen their understanding of game development principles, and broaden their horizons in the realm of web-based gaming. Through hands-on participation and collaboration, members have the unique opportunity to gain practical experience, learn from one another, and continually push the boundaries of their abilities.
